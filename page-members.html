Membership

There are lots of benefits to being a member of Mid Sussex Tri Club, for one you get to hang out with nice people!
Apart from that we are affiliated to a number of official sporty organisations, have regular training sessions and loads of resources for you to tap into.

How can I join?

If you would like to join please

  • Download a membership form [LINK] and complete it
  • Make a payment to the club by bank transfer or cheque (details are on the form)
  • Return the form to the membership secretary via email or in person if paying by cheque

Membership costs

  • Individual (18 or above): £30
  • Couple: £50
  • Unemployed / Full time student: £20
  • Swims subs: £30 for 6 months

Membership runs from April to April
Half season membership is offered at 50% discount if signing up after 1st August.
